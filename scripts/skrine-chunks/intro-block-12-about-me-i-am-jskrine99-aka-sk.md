# Intro – ABOUT ME: I am JSKRINE99 aka Skrine aka LeBron Jam…

ABOUT ME: I am JSKRINE99 aka Skrine aka LeBron James aka Bronny James Jr. aka Tingus Pingus. I have been playing zombies since WaW in 2008 and have been playing non-stop since. I joined the zombie record community in early 2019. I actually started by doing EE speedruns on IX with my friends. On 14/3/2019, I got my first world record... IX 4p EE speedrun (all elixers). From here, I have been striving to become better on every game. I have a world record on almost every game and have one for practically every record type. Playing all these games and records allowed me to meet so many incredible people. Learning from the best in the world has always been a great time. In the middle of 2023, I peaked with around 415 world records (most by any one player at one time). Since then, I kind of stopped playing zombies but have been able to snag some records with the homies here and there. At this point in my career, I want to spend my time spreading my knowledge to all those who want it. I, as well as everyone else, was once a new guy learning the ropes from the pros. Now, I am able to help those who are just starting their journey. Special shoutout to KXG124 for being an information powerhouse and inspiring me to learn as much as possible about everything.
